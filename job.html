APPLICATION & VERIFICATION FEE

A standardized, non-refundable fee is required to apply for employment or partnership opportunities at the Presidential Center. This fee covers essential verification and administrative costs.

What the Fee Is Used For:

  • Identity verification and background checks
  • Processing of applications and documents
  • Maintaining a transparent and accountable selection process

Please Note:

  • Payment of the fee does not guarantee employment or partnership.
  • The fee is the same for all applicants to ensure fairness.

FEE: $500 (Five Hundred Dollars Only)
